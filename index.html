MASTERMIND!

INSTRUCTIONS:

Your goal is to break the 4 digit code that stands between you and saving the world from nuclear disaster! Click inside the empty circle to cycle through the 6 possible colors and leave a color in each circle. Once you have chosen your four colors you may click the “crack the code” button to submit your guess. On the right hand side of the board your nemesis will respond with filling the circles according to two distinctions. Grey if you got one of your colors correct. (One peg per correct color even if the code contains the color twice). Black is returned if you got a color correct and the position correct. From there, take your next educated guess and submit another guess. The game will continue accordingly until you crack the code / save the world or face nuclear holocaust! Don’t let fate become a reality!
